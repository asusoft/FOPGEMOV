Our Story

FOPGEMOV, Non-Profit Organization

FOPGEMOV is a non-profit and non-political non-governmental organization with head office in Damaturu Yobe State Capital. FOPGEMOV is aimed at promoting gender equity, ethics, and moral values and as well promoting peaceful and violence free societal co-existence, proper sanitation and hygiene across schools and communities in the BAY states and largely Northeast Nigeria. The organization was formally registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) on 23rd day of July 2014. FOPGEMOV to-date maintains cordial working relationships with the states’ owned regulatory MDAs, networks and other developmental and humanitarian sectors and sub-sectors partnerships. The organization through its all-inclusive programs and projects support the society in the areas of shelter, healthcare services, WASH, education, protection, empowerment, and motivational trends towards knowledge and skills acquisition. One of the outstanding performance and achievement of the organization is supporting and upgrading the living standards among orphans, widows, children, less privilege of the society, people living with disability, people affected by crisis and other disasters, targeting the girl-child as a concern by developing multifaceted programs and partnerships to address poverty, hunger, violence, education and social skills as major obstacles to the realization of fundamental rights in the society especially within the rural communities. As an NGO, FOPGEMOV also partners with other NGOs in complementing the efforts of government with regards to the socio-economic and welfare incentives it offers to the community by educating children and youth of the society and providing them with the much needed moral upbringing.

Our Mission

To promote gender equity, ethics and moral values by facilitating sustainable societal growth and development through all-inclusive community engagements.

Our Vision

To have a peaceful and tolerant society where GENDER is not a barricade to achieve and showcase individual potential, and to participate in all life endeavours.
